This is the Starfield project! This was mainly done by taking a little bit of code from Dr. R that made objects move, and then making multiple different particles from it. There are 3 types of particles, Normal, Oddball, and Jumbo. The Normal particles move clockwise while the oddball particles move counter clockwise. The Oddball particles are one layer in front of the Normal ones. The Oddballs are also a deep shade of purple that gives the project a very trippy look to it. Finally the Jumbo particle is just a picture of Dr. R's face.
